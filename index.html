ABOUT US

We Provide enhanced
health care technology
Since 2013

Stonymed Technical Services Limited is a professional medical and laboratory services company. Established in 2013, we have been in the health care industry providing technical solutions to our esteemed clients according to international best practice in the areas of supply, installation and maintenance of medical and laboratory equipments and consumables, which has improved health care service delivery by our clients. With our headquarters in Area II, Garki - Abuja, Nigeria, we stand tall with our team of highly qualified and well trained biomedical experts. Our impressive services has given us a wide spread in major tertiary health care centres across Nigeria. This is on account of our ability to provide quality technical solutions that is highly sustainable and cost effective.

Our Vision

Our Vision is to provide enhanced healthcare technology to Hospitals in Nigeria and the boundaries of Africa. To achieve this, we partner with renowned manufacturers in the field of biomedical engineering and equipment technology for the supply and maintenance of high-quality medical devices, with brands that conform to international standards such as ISO, FDA.
