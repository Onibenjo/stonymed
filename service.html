Picture Archiving & Communication Systems PACS

Picture archiving and communication system (PACS) is a modality of imaging technology which helps in image transmission from the site of image acquisition to physically separate device/locations. At Stonymed Technical Services LTD, we install PAC systems for our clients for the storage and retrieval of their computed tomography images and magnetic resonance images..
